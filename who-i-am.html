Hi, I'm Chris...

Cahbreis Designs is a company of one. My name is Chris Cabe and I am a web/graphic designer. I enjoy creating pretty things. I wrestle, sometimes to my detriment, with the many elements of a design project always striving to transform a good design into an exceptional design. I like mulling over several different fonts in search of a collection that hits the "sweet spot" and feels right. I do the same with color varieties and combinations as well as patterns and shading. I love piecing it all together; making a miriade of singular elements come together and sing in harmony with one another. To that end, I've listed a variety of projects below that I feel lend themselves to the talent I possess.
